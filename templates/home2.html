About

Hi there! I'm Adelle. Welcome to my corner of the interwebs.

I'm a girl from the Philippines, and I have lived in Southeast Asia my entire life, having spent the first thirteen years of my life living in Myanmar before moving to the Philippines for four years during high school.

I now set up camp in the USA, where I attend Princeton University as a proud member of the Class of 2019, studying for my BSE in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering. Go Tigers!

One of my biggest personal values is community service. I want to dedicate my life to creating and distributing technology that has a positive social impact, serving a community in need in some type of way. What shape or form that will take, time will tell! But I am extremely excited to see where life will lead me.

My biggest hobbies are drawing, creative writing, programming, and Pokémon. And eating's up there too. Love eating.
